From Gentle Beginnings to Intricate Illusions

The journey begins with simpler patterns that are easier to reveal.
As you progress:

  • Intermediate

    Patterns become denser

    The shapes are still clear, but the texture grows richer.
    Your eyes begin to understand the trick — depth no longer feels accidental.

  • Advanced

    Hidden objects become more complex

    Forms start blending into the pattern.
    You don’t just see the illusion — you search for it, and suddenly it clicks.

  • Master

    Depth perception becomes subtler

    The illusion doesn’t shout.
    It whispers.
    Only a trained focus reveals what lies beneath.

They ease you into it pretty well - you won't feel totally lost starting out before exploring more intricate illusions.
Each level gets you a little better at spotting these things.
