See Beyond the Surface

Stereograms, often known as “Magic Eye” images, are these patterns that somehow manage to hide 3D stuff in what looks like wallpaper. By adjusting your focus — either relaxing your eyes or shifting your depth perception — hidden shapes begin to emerge.

  • Relax Your Eyes

    Let your gaze soften. Don't focus on the surface pattern.

  • Look Through the Image

    Imagine you're looking at something far behind the screen.

  • The Magic Appears

    A 3D shape emerges from the flat pattern, floating in space.
